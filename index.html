How to Prepare for Election

  • Check your registration (COMELEC website)
  • Know your precinct location ahead of time
  • Make a list of candidates to vote for (bring a kodigo)
  • Follow voting guidelines to avoid ballot rejection

Voting Wisely Matters

  • Know your candidates: background, credentials, track record
  • Choose values over popularity
  • Say NO to vote buying
  • Every vote counts, especially yours
  • Beware of fake news and misinformation

Do's and Don'ts When Voting

Do's

  • Research the candidates thoroughly.
  • Bring a valid ID.
  • Follow the official voting process.

Don'ts

  • Sell your vote.
  • Campaign within the polling station.
  • Take a photo of your ballot.
